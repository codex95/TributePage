Prophet Muhammad

The Final Messenger

Time line of Prophet Muhammad's life:

  • 570 - Death of his father, Abdullah
  • 570 - Born in Mecca, Arabia
  • 577 - Death of his mother, Amina
  • 583 - His grandfather transfers him to Syria
  • 595 - Meets and marries Khadijah
  • 599 - Birth of Zainab, his first daughter, followed by: Ruqayyah, Umm Kulthum, and Fatima Zahra
  • 610 - Qur'anic revelation begins in the Cave of Hira on the Jabal an-Nour, the "Mountain of Light" near Mecca. At age 40, Angel Jebreel (Gabriel) was said to appear to Muhammad on the mountain and call him "the Prophet of Allah". Begins in secret to gather followers in Mecca
  • 613 - Begins spreading message of Islam publicly to all Meccans
  • 614 - Heavy persecution of Muslims begins
  • 615 - Emigration of a group of Muslims to Ethiopia
  • 616 - Banu Hashim clan boycott begins
  • 619 - Banu Hashim clan boycott ends. The year of sorrows: Khadija (his wife) and Abu Talib (his uncle) die
  • 620 - Isra and Mi'raj (reported ascension to heaven to meet God)
  • 622 - Hijra, emigration to Medina (called Yathrib)
  • 624 - Battle of Badr
  • 625 - Battle of Uhud
  • 627 - Battle of the Trench (also known as the siege of Medina)
  • 628 - The Meccan tribe of Quraysh and the Muslim community in Medina sign a 10-year truce called the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah
  • 630 - Conquest of Mecca
  • 632 - Farewell pilgrimage, event of Ghadir Khumm, and death, in what is now Saudi Arabia

"I have studied him – the wonderful man and in my opinion far from being an anti-Christ, he must be called the Savior of Humanity. I believe that if a man like him were to assume the dictatorship of the modern world he would succeed in solving its problems in a way that would bring it the much needed peace and happiness.""

-- Sir George Bernard Shaw in ‘The Genuine Islam,’ Vol. 1, No. 8, 1936.
